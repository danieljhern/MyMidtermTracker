My Washington Midterms Tracker


Welcome to your Washington Midterms Tracker. Here you can learn about the candidates running for election to Congress in each of Washington's 10 districts. Please see the below for detail on each tab's use.


Distrticts: The Districts page will allow you to view a list of Candidates by District

Add Custom Candidate: You can add a candidate not represented in the list in the Add Custom Candidate page

List: The List page displays a list of all your tracked candidates and enables you to add/adjust notes or remove candidates from your list

Resources: The Resources page contains links to register to vote and polling locations

2022 Washington Midterms


The 2022 U.S. House of Representatives elections in Washington will take place on November 8, 2022. Voters will elect 10 candidates to serve in the U.S. House, one from each of the state's 10 congressional districts. To see a full list of candidates in the primary for each district, click "Select District" below.
